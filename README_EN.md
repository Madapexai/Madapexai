## What I Believe

**The essence of management is being rebuilt.** From managing people to managing Agents, from instructions to loops, from supervision to self-evolution. This is not a tool upgrade — it is a leap in the organizational paradigm.

**Loop > Prompt.** Writing one good Loop is worth more than a hundred Prompts. Loops are reusable, evolvable, and auditable; a Prompt is just a one-off instruction.

**Practice is the only test.** These three books are not conceptual exercises — they are methodologies distilled from running real Agent teams. Every method has been stress-tested, broken, and validated.
